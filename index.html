◆ DO
  • Use Aegis Orange for primary CTAs and the brand mark only.
  • Reserve Signal Volt for live alerts, status indicators, and AR overlays.
  • Default surfaces to Shield Obsidian — let the brand colors do the talking.
◆ DON'T
  • Never tint Aegis Orange or Signal Volt — they're absolute, not gradients.
  • Don't pair Orange and Volt as adjacent fills; they're meant to take turns.
  • Don't use Volt for body text — it's signal-only, never reading copy.

04 · VOICE

Fearless.
Precise.

We speak in short, declarative sentences. We never panic. When the situation is critical, the words get shorter — not louder.

§ BRAND PERSONALITY
Innovative Trustworthy Sharp Fearless Precise
§ WRITING RULES
  • Lead with the action. "Drone dispatched" — not "We've dispatched a drone."
  • Numbers earn their place. If a stat is shown, it's accurate to its precision.
  • Calm in crisis. Critical alerts use fewer words, larger type — never exclamation marks.
  • Plain over clever. "Stay put. Help is coming." not "Hang tight, cavalry's en route."
